Welcome to PX2 Connect

Efficiently manage and monitor your PX2 devices
Direct Connection
Connect to PX2 devices using the default network address (169.254.1.1) or your stored custom addresses.
Fast Access
Quick keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+Shift+1 for default address, Ctrl+Shift+2/3 for custom addresses.
Theme Selection
Choose your preferred interface theme - Dark or Light mode for comfortable device management.
Recovery Tools
Built-in rescue mode for device maintenance, recovery, and firmware updates.
